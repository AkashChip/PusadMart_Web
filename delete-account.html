Delete Your PusadMart Account

PusadMart respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by following the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at pusadmartstore@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: “Account Deletion Request”
  3. Include your registered mobile number in the email

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (name, phone number)
  • Saved addresses
  • Authentication data

Data That May Be Retained

  • Order history and invoices (for legal and tax compliance)
  • Transaction records (retained as required by law)

Retained data is securely stored and will be deleted after the legally required retention period.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days.

If you have any questions, contact us at pusadmartstore@gmail.com.
