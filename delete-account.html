PusadMart - Delete Account

Effective Date: 12 December 2025

At PusadMart, you have the right to delete your account and associated data. Please follow the instructions below to request account deletion.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at pusadmartstore@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered mobile number (used for login) in the email

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (name, phone number, email)
  • Saved addresses
  • Authentication data (OTP records, login tokens)

Data That May Be Retained

  • Order history and invoices (for legal and tax compliance)
  • Transaction records (retained as required by law)

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days.
