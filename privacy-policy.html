PusadMart - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12 December 2025

Welcome to PusadMart. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app or website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, mobile number, email address.
  • Location Details: Delivery address for order fulfillment.
  • Payment Information: UPI/Wallet/Online payment details (handled securely by payment gateway; we do not store card details).
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, app usage data.
  • Order Details: Items ordered, transaction history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To verify your identity using OTP-based authentication.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information. We may share data only with:

  • Delivery partners to deliver orders.

  • Government or legal authorities when required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or analytics tools to improve app performance and user experience.

  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Update or correct your information.
