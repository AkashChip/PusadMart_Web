PusadMart - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16 December 2025

Welcome to PusadMart. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our app or website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, mobile number, email address.
  • Location Details: Delivery address provided by you for order fulfillment.
  • Payment Information: UPI, wallet, or online payment details handled securely by third-party payment gateways. We do not store credit or debit card details.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version, and app usage data.
  • Order Details: Items ordered, transaction history.
  • App Activity: In-app interactions and search history to improve user experience.
  • Identifiers: App-generated user ID and device identifiers for authentication and notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To verify your identity using OTP-based authentication.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To send order updates, promotions, and notifications.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To analyze app usage and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information. We may share data only with:

  • Delivery partners for order fulfillment.
  • Payment gateways for secure transactions.
  • Service providers such as SMS/OTP providers, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including encryption in transit, to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and analytics tools to analyze usage, personalize content, and improve app performance. No personally identifiable payment information is shared with advertisers.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal and order information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Deleted accounts are removed within 7 days, except for transaction records which are retained for legal and tax compliance (up to 7 years).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Update or correct your information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data via our Delete Account page.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect device identifiers, app usage data, and crash reports. No personally identifiable payment information is shared with these services.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
